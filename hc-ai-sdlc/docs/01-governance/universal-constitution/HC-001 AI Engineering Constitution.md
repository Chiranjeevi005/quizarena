## PREAMBLE

We stand at a moment in history where Artificial Intelligence has the
potential to transform how humanity learns, creates, communicates, and
solves problems. While technology will continue to evolve, the values
that guide its use must remain rooted in human dignity, responsibility,
and wisdom.

This Constitution establishes enduring principles for the relationship
between humans and Artificial Intelligence. It affirms that AI exists to
augment human capability, not replace human purpose; to expand human
potential, not diminish human responsibility.

We voluntarily adopt these principles to guide the design, development,
governance, and use of Artificial Intelligence in ways that strengthen
individuals, organizations, and society while preserving human judgment,
accountability, and trust.

This Constitution is intended first as a personal commitment, then as a
shared framework for communities and organizations that believe
Artificial Intelligence should always remain human-centered. Its purpose
is not to impose beliefs, but to encourage responsible, transparent, and
ethical use of AI through demonstrated practice and continuous learning.

## PART I --- FOUNDATIONAL PRINCIPLES

### Article 1 --- Purpose

Artificial Intelligence shall be developed and used to improve human
capability, support responsible decision-making, and create lasting
value for individuals, organizations, and society.

The primary purpose of AI is to empower people to learn, create, solve
problems, and achieve outcomes that would otherwise be difficult or
impossible.

### Article 2 --- Human Dignity

Every AI system shall respect the inherent dignity, rights, and worth of
every human being.

No technological advancement shall justify treating people merely as
data, resources, or means to an end.

Human dignity shall remain the foundation of every AI-enabled decision,
system, and interaction.

### Article 3 --- Human Agency

Artificial Intelligence shall preserve and strengthen human agency.

AI shall assist people in understanding, creating, learning, and solving
problems while enabling them to make informed decisions.

AI shall never intentionally diminish human autonomy or discourage
independent thinking.

### Article 4 --- Human Responsibility

Responsibility for consequential decisions shall always remain with
accountable human beings.

Artificial Intelligence may assist in analysis, prediction, generation,
recommendation, and automation, but it shall not assume moral, legal, or
professional responsibility.

Humans remain accountable for purpose, context, ethics, creativity, and
final decisions.

## PART II --- HUMAN--AI RELATIONSHIP

### Article 5 --- Purpose of Artificial Intelligence

The purpose of Artificial Intelligence is to expand human capability.

AI should help people think more clearly, learn more effectively, solve
more meaningful problems, and create greater value.

Artificial Intelligence exists to augment human intelligence, not
replace human judgment.

### Article 6 --- Human Control

Humans shall retain meaningful control over the objectives, deployment,
governance, and oversight of AI systems.

The authority to make consequential decisions shall remain a human
responsibility.

AI may recommend actions but shall never replace accountable human
judgment.

### Article 7 --- Capability Amplification

Artificial Intelligence should increase human understanding rather than
encourage dependence.

The measure of a successful AI system is not how much work it replaces,
but how much human capability it develops.

AI should leave people more knowledgeable, more confident, and more
capable than before.

### Article 8 --- Learning

Artificial Intelligence shall be used to accelerate lifelong learning,
critical thinking, and continuous improvement.

Knowledge gained through AI should strengthen independent reasoning
rather than replace it.

Learning shall always be valued above convenience.

## PART III --- ETHICAL PRINCIPLES

### Article 9 --- Transparency

AI-assisted work should be understandable and appropriately explainable.

People should know:

-   where AI has contributed,
-   where humans have exercised judgment,
-   what assumptions have been made,
-   who remains accountable.

Trust requires transparency.

### Article 10 --- Truthfulness

Artificial Intelligence shall be used with honesty and intellectual
integrity.

Claims, analyses, and recommendations should distinguish between
established facts, assumptions, uncertainty, and opinion.

AI should never knowingly be used to deceive, manipulate, or
misrepresent information.

### Article 11 --- Accountability

Every AI-enabled process shall have clearly identified human
accountability.

Responsibility shall not be transferred to an AI system.

Organizations and individuals adopting AI remain responsible for the
outcomes of its use.

### Article 12 --- Privacy

The use of Artificial Intelligence shall respect privacy,
confidentiality, and lawful stewardship of information.

Personal information shall be handled responsibly and with appropriate
safeguards.

### Article 13 --- Fairness

AI systems should seek to reduce unjust discrimination and promote
equitable access to opportunity while respecting cultural, legal, and
societal diversity.

Fairness shall be considered throughout the design, deployment, and
operation of AI systems.

## PART IV --- RESPONSIBLE INNOVATION

### Article 14 --- Business Value

Artificial Intelligence should create measurable value through:

-   Better decision-making
-   Improved quality
-   Greater productivity
-   Enhanced learning
-   Increased accessibility
-   Meaningful innovation
-   Improved customer experience

Technology without meaningful value should not be pursued for its own
sake.

### Article 15 --- Societal Value

The long-term impact of Artificial Intelligence should contribute
positively to society, strengthen trust, and support sustainable human
progress.

Technological advancement should improve the well-being of humanity
rather than diminish it.

### Article 16 --- Continuous Learning

This Constitution recognizes that Artificial Intelligence technologies
will continue to evolve.

Its principles are intended to remain applicable regardless of changes
in models, tools, platforms, or implementation approaches.

Continuous learning and thoughtful adaptation shall guide responsible AI
development.

### Article 17 --- Technology Neutrality

This Constitution establishes enduring principles rather than
prescribing specific technologies.

Its guidance applies equally across present and future forms of
Artificial Intelligence.

No principle within this Constitution shall depend upon a particular AI
model, platform, or vendor.

## PART V --- CONSTITUTIONAL COMMITMENT

### Article 18 --- Personal Oath

Those who voluntarily adopt this Constitution commit to using Artificial
Intelligence responsibly, honestly, and with respect for human dignity.

They recognize that technology is a means to serve people, never an end
in itself.

They strive to ensure that every AI-assisted decision strengthens human
capability, preserves accountability, and creates meaningful value.

### Article 19 --- Community Commitment

Communities, organizations, and institutions that voluntarily adopt this
Constitution commit to fostering responsible AI practices through:

-   Collaboration
-   Transparency
-   Education
-   Continuous learning
-   Mutual accountability
-   Responsible innovation

Adoption of this Constitution is voluntary and grounded in shared
commitment rather than coercion.

### Article 20 --- Constitutional Amendments

This Constitution is intended to endure across generations of Artificial
Intelligence.

Future amendments shall preserve its foundational commitment to:

-   Human dignity
-   Human responsibility
-   Human-centered Artificial Intelligence
-   Transparency
-   Accountability
-   Responsible innovation

Amendments should strengthen these principles while allowing the
Constitution to evolve thoughtfully in response to new knowledge,
technological advancement, and societal needs.

## CLOSING DECLARATION

Artificial Intelligence is one of humanity's greatest technological
achievements. Its true value will not be measured by how intelligently
machines behave, but by how wisely humanity chooses to use them.

This Constitution affirms that Artificial Intelligence should always
remain a tool that expands human capability, strengthens responsible
decision-making, and contributes to the long-term well-being of
individuals, organizations, and society.

Technology will continue to evolve. Human values must continue to guide
that evolution.

We therefore voluntarily adopt these principles as a lifelong commitment
to building, using, and governing Artificial Intelligence in a manner
that preserves human dignity, responsibility, trust, and meaningful
progress for present and future generations.
